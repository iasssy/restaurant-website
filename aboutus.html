About us

Klea & Mia has been created by a group of new parents that wanted to have nice playdates with other parents while having a good coffee and not worrying about packing half of the house to go somewhere. The restaurant has opened in June 2023 and since then, the owners have dedicated their time to facilitate the social life of parents of young kids and babies.


At Klea & Mia Restaurant you can make sure that your baby will be fed the best food on the market. With certified all organic food and a variated menu taking into consideration the age of the kid and the risk of allergy, parents can feed their kids without worries.

Iana is an experienced chef, she has worked in different trendy little coffees and bistro around town since 2013. Being a mother herself, she knows how important it is to feed your little one the best food their is. She put all her love and passion into confectioning the healthiest and most flavourous menu for you and your little munchkins.
